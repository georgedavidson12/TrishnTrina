Welcome to Trish N Trina

Founded in 1983 by two sisters, Trish and Trina, our academy began as tap and disco class in Hetton-Le-Hole. Over the decades, we've grown into a community where students of all ages not only learn to dance — they become part of the TNT family. From local classes to international stages, our journey continues with energy, heart, and a love of performance that hasn’t faded in over 40 years.
